About US

In India, a significant number of unclaimed dead bodies report to hospital and health authorities. The epidemiology of these unclaimed dead bodies and various methods to identify them has been studied by various forensic experts but no study has considered pitfalls in dignified disposal of the dead bodies.Our vision is to provide a dignified funeral to unclaimed dead bodies. We intend to act as a mediator among the person who has come across a orphaned dead body and the firm by creating an efficacious app that provides the location and other details of the dead bodies, so as to notify the people who aim to perform the last rites of the dead.
